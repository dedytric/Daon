Slide 2: Market Opportunity Snapshot
(Focus: 280M+ Population, >$150B Digital Economy, 300+ Fintechs)

"Simply put, the numbers speak for themselves. Indonesia is a colossal market, boasting over 280 million people and a digital economy projected to exceed $150 billion by 2025. This isn't just a large population; it’s a fully mobilized digital population."

"More importantly, the financial sector is going through a massive transformation—we're looking at 300+ banks and fintechs racing to digitize their onboarding. This rapid shift, coupled with the rise of deepfakes and tighter data compliance laws, creates a critical, urgent need for robust, regulatory-compliant eKYC. The window is open, and we need to be the first global identity player to seize it."

Slide 3: Daon Value Proposition
(Focus: IdentityX/TrustX, Anti-Spoofing, Global Compliance)

"Here's why Daon is the perfect fit. Local enterprises often struggle with fragmented, siloed verification systems. Our IdentityX and TrustX platform offers the clean, end-to-end orchestration they desperately need from a single vendor."

"Our key competitive edge is trust and security. Our proven biometrics tackle the sophisticated threat of deepfakes using regulatory-grade anti-spoofing and passive liveness, which very few can match. Plus, our 20+ years of proven deployments with global Tier-1 clients instantly gives us the credibility and local compliance confidence that a new vendor simply cannot achieve alone."

Slide 4: Market Penetration Model — Partner-Led Approach
(Focus: Boiva, RDS, Telkom Group)

"We're not building a large office first; we're using a Partner-Led Model to minimize CAPEX and maximize speed. We've lined up three strategic pillars who are already embedded in the market:"

Boiva (Fintech): They give us immediate API access to the high-growth digital banking and lending ecosystem. They are agile and plugged in.

RDS (Insurance & BFSI): They are our trusted channel for high-compliance institutions like major banks and insurers. They handle the complex, document-heavy onboarding.

Telkom Group: This is our Strategic Anchor. Partnering with a Telkom entity like Telkomsigma gives us direct access to GovTech projects and the core infrastructure, which is essential for long-term strategic growth."

Slide 5: Expansion Strategy (3-Track Model)
(Focus: T1: Fintech/PPV, T2: Insurance/Projects, T3: GovTech/Contracts)

"To ensure balanced and resilient growth, we're executing a simultaneous attack across all three high-value verticals:"

Track 1 (Fintech): This is our volume play. It's all about high-frequency, low-cost Pay-Per-Verification (PPV) recurring revenue through e-wallets and digital banks.

Track 2 (Insurance): This is our stable income track. It focuses on higher-value project integration fees for complex authentication and claim processes.

Track 3 (Government): This is our big-ticket, long-term track. With Telkom, we aim for large, multi-year contract agreements, starting with the National Digital ID PoC. This validates us at the highest level."

Slide 6: Strategic Initiative: Leverage Global Relationships for Local Entry
(Focus: Standard Chartered/Citi, AIA/Prudential)

"We don’t need to knock on cold doors. We're initiating a Global-to-Local Bridge program. We will activate our existing global relationships with clients like Standard Chartered, Citi, AIA, and Mastercard to bring their IdentityX deployments to their Indonesian subsidiaries."

"This strategy provides instantaneous Tier-1 validation, bypassing the early sales cycles and proving our technology locally using names management already trusts globally. This dramatically accelerates our time-to-market."

Slide 7: Strategic Execution Plan
(Focus: Map, Bridge, Execute)

"Our execution plan is laser-focused and three-pronged:"

Map: We start by prioritizing our global clients who are already operating in Indonesia.

Bridge: We finalize product localization and develop powerful, concise Global-to-Local success case studies to show the local teams what the global team already uses.

Execute: All sales and implementation are handled by our trusted channel partners. We will employ a 'Daon Inside' co-branding strategy to quickly build brand awareness while relying on the partners' local trust and compliance frameworks."
